Applied Super Powers was founded in 2045 by a group of scientists who decided that the only way to make the world a better place was to find a way to develop a serum that would endow a user with super-human abilities that was once thought to never exist back in the age where such theories were thought to be found only in comic books and movies. After countless failed formulas, the scientists ran out of test subjects, so they began experimenting on each other. Finally, after the majority of them either burst into flames or exploded from an improper balance of chemicals in their body caused by the serum, their experiment became a success. The result now has one of them endowed with the ability of super strength.
